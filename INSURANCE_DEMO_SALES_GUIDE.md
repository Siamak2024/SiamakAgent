- **Competition**: Guidewire, Duck Creek, Accenture

### The EA Engagement

**Complete E0-E5 workflow execution:**

### Act 1: The Account View (7 minutes)

1. **Start at Growth Dashboard**
   ```

   ```

3. **Navigate through tabs**
   - **Opportunities Tab**: Show the €2.5M Digital Modernization opportunity

     ```
     compliance concerns. Our engagement strategy: involve her in risk assessment reviews."
     ```
   - **AI Insights Tab**: (If implemented) Generate account analysis

### Act 2: The Opportunity Pipeline (5 minutes)

**Objective**: Show how EA insights shape opportunities

1. **Click breadcrumb "Home" → Navigate to Opportunity Pipeline**
   ```
   "Let's see this opportunity in our pipeline. It's in 'Propose' stage - we're presenting next week."
   ```

2. **Show opportunity details**
   - **Value**: €2.5M (but tied to larger €5M+ technology spend)
   - **Probability**: 70% (high because of our EA work)
   - **Next Steps**: Executive presentation April 25, design workshop May 2-4
   - **Linked to Value Case**: €6.1M quantified business value
   
   ```
   "Our EA engagement is directly shaping this opportunity. We've identified €6.1M in business value 
   over 3 years. That makes our €2.5M advisory fee an easy sell."
   ```

### Act 3: The EA Engagement Workflow (12 minutes)

**Objective**: Demonstrate structured methodology and depth of work

1. **Navigate to EA Engagement Playbook**
   ```
   "Now let me show you HOW we created this value. Our EA engagement follows a proven 6-phase 
   methodology: E0 through E5. Let me walk through what we've done..."
   ```

2. **E0: Initiation (2 min)**
   - Show **Scope**: "Digital insurance platform covering customer channels, API layer, claims, policy admin"
   - Show **5 Success Criteria**: Including "Business case showing >200% ROI" ✅
   - Show **5 Stakeholders**: Mapped with engagement strategies
   - Show **4 Constraints**: e.g., "99.95% uptime required", "GDPR compliance mandatory"
   - Show **4 Key Decisions**: e.g., "Adopt Azure cloud", "API-first integration"
   
   ```
   "In E0, we established clear scope and governance. We identified Lisa Anderson, the CRO, as 
   resistant due to regulatory concerns. Our strategy: make compliance a core design principle."
   ```

3. **E1: Analysis (3 min)**
   - Show **6 Legacy Applications**: 
     * Policy Admin System (2003, mainframe, score: 1/10, status: RETIRE)
     * Claims Processing (manual, 14-day avg, status: RETIRE)
   - Show **7 Capability Gaps**:
     * Customer Self-Service: Current=2, Target=9, Gap=7 (HIGH priority)
     * Real-time Underwriting: Current=1, Target=8, Gap=7
   - Show **4 Key Risks**: e.g., "Data migration from 20-year mainframe"
   
   ```
   "E1 is where we documented their AS-IS mess. Their policy system is from 2003 - literally COBOL 
   on a mainframe. We assessed 6 applications and identified 7 critical capability gaps. This 
   analysis gave us the ammunition to justify transformation."
   ```

4. **E2: Target Architecture (3 min)**
   - Show **6 Strategic Initiatives**:
     * API Gateway & Integration Platform (€450K, 6 months)
     * Native Mobile App (€680K, 9 months)
     * Cloud Migration to Azure (€920K, 18 months)
     * Cloud-Native Claims Platform with AI (€1.1M, 12 months)
     * Policy Admin Modernization - Guidewire (€2.4M, 15 months)
     * Customer Data Platform & Analytics (€580K, 10 months)
   
   ```
   "E2 is the target state. We've designed 6 strategic initiatives totaling €6.1M in investment.
   Notice the sequencing: API layer first, enabling mobile app and cloud migration in parallel.
   This phased approach reduces risk and shows early value."
   ```

5. **E3: Roadmap (2 min)**
   - Show **24-Month Roadmap**: 3 phases
     * Phase 1 (Months 1-9): Digital Channels & API Layer
     * Phase 2 (Months 7-19): Claims Modernization
     * Phase 3 (Months 13-28): Policy Admin Migration
   
   ```
   "E3 is the sequenced roadmap. We've planned 24 months with clear milestones. Phase 1 delivers 
   mobile app in 9 months - that's their #1 customer pain point addressed quickly."
   ```

6. **E4: Business Case (2 min)**
   - Show **Value Case**:
     * Total Investment: €7.4M over 3 years
     * Total Benefits: €13.5M
     * Net Benefit: €6.1M
     * ROI: 82%
     * Payback Period: 2.1 years
   - Show **6 Value Drivers**:
     1. Increase digital sales 12% → 40% = €2.8M
     2. Reduce claims cost 60% (automation) = €2.1M
     3. Cloud cost savings 35% = €1.4M
     4. Improve retention +5% (NPS improvement) = €1.9M
     5. Faster product launch (3mo → 2wk) = €1.2M
     6. Call center volume -30% = €0.9M
   
   ```
   "This is where EA becomes business value. We've quantified €13.5M in benefits against €7.4M 
   investment. The CDO and CIO have approved; we're waiting on CFO and CEO sign-off.
   
   Notice the value drivers are specific and measurable. Not fluffy 'better customer experience' -
   we're saying '40% digital sales penetration' and 'reduce claims processing from 14 days to 48 hours'."
   ```

## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Before the Demo
1. **Practice the flow** - Run through the demo 2-3 times to be smooth
2. **Customize talking points** - Use your company's terminology (e.g., "strategic accounts" vs. "enterprise customers")
3. **Load demo data** - Pre-load SecureLife scenario before the meeting
4. **Test all features** - Verify search, analytics dashboard, exports work
5. **Prepare backup** - Have screenshots in case of technical issues

### During the Demo
1. **Start with the problem** - Lead with SecureLife's pain points (14-day claims, 2.1-star mobile app)
2. **Connect to revenue** - Emphasize €2.5M opportunity shaped by EA work
3. **Show, don't tell** - Click through the platform; avoid talking over slides
4. **Pause for questions** - After each Act, ask "Questions on this part?"
5. **Use global search** - Demonstrates interconnectedness of data

### Handling Common Questions

**Q: "How long does an engagement take?"**
> "E0-E2 typically 8-12 weeks. SecureLife is at week 6, with E2 at 85% complete. 
> Full E0-E5 depends on scope - this one is 6 months given the transformation complexity."

**Q: "Can we customize the methodology?"**
> "Absolutely. E0-E5 is a framework, not a straitjacket. For smaller opportunities, 
> we can run a lightweight version (E0-E2 only). For strategic accounts like SecureLife, 
> we execute the full methodology."

**Q: "What if the customer doesn't have budget for EA advisory?"**
> "Our AS-IS assessment (E1) often uncovers so much value that it creates the budget. 
> SecureLife initially had no budget for transformation. Our capability gap analysis showed 
> they're losing €4M/year in inefficiency. That funded the €7.4M investment immediately."

**Q: "How does this integrate with our CRM (Salesforce/Dynamics)?"**
> "Currently it's standalone, using browser localStorage. We have integrations on roadmap 
> (Phase 6) to sync with Salesforce opportunities, Dynamics accounts, and JIRA for delivery 
> tracking. For now, it's a specialized EA tool that complements CRM."

**Q: "Can multiple people work on the same engagement?"**
> "Yes - the platform supports multi-user via localStorage export/import. For real-time 
> collaboration, Phase 6 will add cloud backend with real-time sync."

**Q: "What about IP protection? Can we white-label this for customers?"**
> "Great question. We can export engagements to PDF/PPTX with your company branding. 
> The methodology (E0-E5) becomes your competitive differentiator. Some consulting firms 
> actually charge for access to their methodology frameworks - this could be monetized."

## 📞 Next Steps After the Demo

### If Sales Leadership is Enthusiastic:
1. **Schedule Training Session** (2 hours)
   - Train account managers on E0-E5 methodology
   - Hands-on: Create their first engagement
   - Assign 2-3 pilot accounts

2. **Create Account-Specific Demos**
   - Work with top 3 account managers
   - Load their real account data (with customer permission)
   - Create tailored demos for their customers

3. **Establish Governance**
   - Define when to engage EA team (e.g., opportunities >€500K)
   - Create service level agreement (SLA) for EA support
   - Set up weekly pipeline reviews using Analytics Dashboard

### If Sales Leadership is Skeptical:
1. **Run a Pilot** (1 strategic account, 90 days)
   - Pick the most challenging account with stuck opportunity
   - Execute E0-E2 engagement
   - Measure: Did opportunity advance? Did deal size increase?

2. **Show Industry Benchmarks**
   - "Gartner says EA-enabled sales cycles are 30% faster..."
   - "Forrester research shows architecture-driven deals are 2.5x larger..."
   - Reference peer companies using similar approaches

3. **Calculate Opportunity Cost**
   - "We have 15 strategic accounts. If EA methodology increases average deal size 
     by just 20%, that's €X additional revenue. Not using this is leaving money on the table."
