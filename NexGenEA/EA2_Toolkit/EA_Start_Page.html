Complete E2E Engagement Process

Six interconnected phases guiding your customer from discovery to value realization

0
E0 - Discover
Initial opportunity identification and account qualification
  • Account profiling
  • Stakeholder mapping
  • Pain point discovery
  • Strategic alignment
  • Opportunity sizing
1
E1 - Assess
Comprehensive current state analysis and capability assessment
  • Application portfolio analysis
  • Capability maturity assessment
  • AI-powered questionnaires
  • Technical architecture review
  • Gap identification
2
E2 - Design
Future state architecture and transformation roadmap
  • Target architecture design
  • Technology selection
  • Transformation roadmap
  • Initiative prioritization
  • Risk & dependency mapping
3
E3 - Propose
Value case development and commercial proposal
  • Financial analysis (NPV, ROI, IRR)
  • Value case development
  • Business case validation
  • Proposal creation
  • Executive presentation
4
E4 - Deliver
Implementation planning and solution delivery
  • Delivery planning
  • Resource allocation
  • Milestone tracking
  • Risk mitigation
  • Change management
5
E5 - Realize
Value tracking and customer success management
  • Value realization tracking
  • Customer Success Plans
  • Executive Business Reviews
  • Expansion opportunities
  • Continuous optimization

Interrelated Activities

  • Stakeholder Engagement: Continuous throughout all phases
  • Team Collaboration: Cross-functional coordination (Sales, EA, Delivery)
  • Data Flow: Account → Opportunities → Engagements → Initiatives
  • Financial Tracking: ROI, NPV, IRR calculations across lifecycle
  • Analytics: Real-time dashboards and KPI monitoring
  • AI Integration: Context-aware recommendations and insights

Isolated/Optional Activities

  • Demo Scenarios: Load Banking, FinTech, or Insurance examples
  • Template Import: Bulk data entry via CSV/JSON
  • Project Export: Standalone project files for sharing
  • Backup/Restore: Independent data snapshots
  • AI Questionnaires: Optional discovery tool per industry
  • KPI Validation: Standalone calculation verification

Key Features

Comprehensive capabilities to support every stage of customer engagement

Account Management
Centralized account tracking with ACV monitoring, health scoring, and pipeline management. CRUD operations with bulk import via CSV/JSON templates.
Opportunity Pipeline
Sales pipeline visualization with stage-based workflow, probability weighting, and quarterly forecasting. Link opportunities to EA engagements.
EA Engagement Workflow
Structured 6-phase engagement model (E0-E5) with deliverables, activities, and stakeholder management at each stage.
Financial Analytics
Complete value case modeling with NPV, ROI, IRR, and Payback calculations. Validated formulas with comprehensive testing suite.
Team Collaboration
Cross-functional account teams with role management, activity logging, and action item tracking across Sales, EA, and Delivery.
AI-Powered Insights
AdvisyAI with layered prompt construction, industry-specific recommendations, and automated questionnaire generation for discovery.
Customer Success
Customer Success Plans with goal tracking, health monitoring, Executive Business Reviews, and value realization metrics.
Analytics Dashboard
Real-time dashboards with pipeline analytics, win rate tracking, forecast modeling, and customizable KPI monitoring.
Data Management
Export/import functionality, backup/restore, template-based bulk import, and demo scenarios for Banking, FinTech, and Insurance.
